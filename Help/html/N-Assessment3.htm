Controller
The AccountController This class will be responsible for taking an user input and sending the approriate response to perform a function related to Accounts
  • AddAccount - Adds Account to Customer
  • AddCustomer - Adds Customer to Customer Repository
  • EditCustomer - Removes old customer with same customerID from Customer Repository and then adds new Customer
  • DeleteCustomer - Removes Customer from Customer Repository
  • Deposit - Credits money to an account
  • Withdraw - Debits money to an account
  • AddInterest - Credits the interest earned by the account
  • Transfer - Transfers money between accounts by debitting one and creditting the other
  • RecordTransaction - Saves the transaction to an Account
